About Me


I started in web development in 2014, but my curiosity the “why” behind products led me to transition into design in 2018.


Since then, I enjoyed taking on challenges of migrating, maintaining, or establishing design systems from scratch, while developing a deep interest in shaping UX governance. Basically putting the UX on top of UX.


I had the privilege of learning from great colleagues and deepend my design system knowledge through blogs by Alice Packard, Diana Mounter, or Nathan Curtis. Outside of work, I also like to connect in Zurich at events like Last Thursday Talks or Friends of Figma.


Thanks to the versatility of UX, I could work on products from both B2C and B2B environments, showcased in the following use cases from selected companies:
